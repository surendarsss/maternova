How Maternova Works

1

Wear & Connect

Simply wear our comfortable, medical-grade wearable device and connect it to the Maternova app on your smartphone. The setup process takes less than 2 minutes.

2

Monitor & Learn

The wearable continuously tracks your vital signs and your baby's activity. Our AI analyzes patterns and provides personalized insights about your pregnancy journey.

3

Share & Prepare

Easily share your health data with healthcare providers and use our smart document tools to prepare all necessary paperwork for your birth plan and hospital stay.

4

Support & Grow

Continue using Maternova after birth to monitor your recovery and your baby's development. Our platform grows with your family's needs.
